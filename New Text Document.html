About KIST

KIST is a popular beverage brand in Sri Lanka, known for its delicious fruit juices and jams. Established by Cargills, KIST offers a range of products made from fresh, high-quality fruits to provide a refreshing taste and retain essential nutrients.

Product Details

  • Manufacturer: Cargills (Ceylon) PLC
  • Product Range: Fruit Juices, Jams, Sauces
  • Flavors Available: Mango, Orange, Mixed Fruit, and more
  • Packaging: Bottles, Cans, and Family Packs
  • Price Range: LKR 50 - 300 depending on size
